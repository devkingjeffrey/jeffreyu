How I Work.

Start With the Real Data

Before any architecture gets designed, I look at what your data actually looks like right now — the spreadsheets, the exports, the inconsistencies — instead of assuming a clean-slate best case that doesn't match reality.

You Stay In Control of Changes

Nothing gets restructured in your systems without you understanding what's changing and why. No black-box migrations — every change is something you could explain back to your own team.

Document As I Go

Every build comes with plain-language notes on what was set up and why, so the system doesn't depend on me being the only person who understands it.

Honest About What's Proven

I'll tell you plainly what's a well-worn pattern versus something we're trying for the first time together — no dressing up a first attempt as a repeatable process.
