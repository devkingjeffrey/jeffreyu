What I Actually Do.

CRM Architecture

Designing and building Salesforce (including Non-Profit Success Pack) and HubSpot instances that hold up as an organization scales — object structure, sharing rules, and pipeline design built around how your team actually works, not a generic template.

Data Reconciliation

Getting donor, partner, and programme data out of parallel spreadsheets and into one consistent, reportable source of truth — the specific problem I focus on across scale-ups, rebrands, and new coalitions.

Workflow Automation

Declarative automation inside Salesforce and HubSpot — routing, notifications, and handoffs that remove manual re-entry, without introducing brittle custom code that only one person can maintain.

Systems Integration

Connecting your CRM to the other tools your team already lives in — Slack, webhooks, reporting layers — so data entered once shows up everywhere it's needed, instead of being copied by hand.

Every capability above is something I actively design and build — not a claim about a team or a delivery track record I don't have yet. See How I Work for the honest version of process, and Case Studies for what this looks like applied.
